## New Business

### 1. Target Audience
- **Primary:** Casual players; social / relaxation-oriented players.  
- **Emphasis:** Fun, entertainment, group interaction, light progression.  
- **De-emphasize:** High-stakes gambling, “winning big,” heavy monetization.  

---

### 2. Research Overview
- Combine key findings into one **research overview** document.  
- **Reuse** existing persona documents rather than creating entirely new personas.  
- **Highlight two main personas** as the primary references.  
- Optionally include other persona materials under **raw research** if helpful.  
- The overview should explain: **who the app is for**, **what features to include**, and **how user needs connect to design choices**.  

---

### 3. Feature Ideas

#### Core gameplay
- Low-cost spins  
- Progression system  
- Random jackpot / event mode  
- Special rewards after a number of spins  
- Different symbol combinations with different outcomes  
- Possible multiplier system  

#### Social / engagement
- Leaderboard  
- Team-based or social play elements  
- Group-friendly design; usable in class or team activities  

#### Customization
- Unlockable themes  
- Skins / emblems  
- Modes such as classic, cartoon, fantasy; light/dark themes  

---

### 4. Visual and Sound Design

#### Visual
- Bright, eye-catching colors  
- **Red** — losses / urgency  
- **Gold/yellow** — wins  
- **Green** — rewards / bonus opportunities  
- **Blue** — calmer, longer-session feel  
- Symbols and visuals should match the selected theme  

#### Layout
- Slot machine remains the **main focus**  
- Reserve space for: progression bar, leaderboard, rates / combination info, other side features  

#### Sound
- Engaging background music  
- Clear win/loss effects  
- Theme-based sounds  
- Possible lever animation or action feedback to make spins feel more engaging  

---

### 5. Legal / Transparency
- Avoid misleading gambling-like behavior.  
- No misleading claims; no false promise of financial success.  
- If monetization exists, explain it clearly.  
- If probabilities are used, communicate rates clearly.  
- Overall: feel like a **fun / themed experience**, not real gambling.  

---

### 6. AI Generation Plan
- Build through **iterative AI prompting**.  
- Produce a **first generation** first, then improve **step by step**.  
- **Iterate on the same run** — not fully separate runs each time.  
- Maintain a **prompt log** recording: prompt used, what changed, and result of each iteration.  
- **First prompt** should stay simple enough to leave room for later design improvements.
